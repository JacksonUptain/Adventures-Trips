What I Consider the Top Ten Greatest Quotes of all Time

No. 10

"A clever person solves a problem.

A wise person avoids it." -Albert Einstien


Why?

I believe that this quote belongs as #10 for a reason. That reason being, that we as humans automatically assume that the only way to move foward is by facing our issues head on. Einstien contradicts this, and says that if we make a wise decision we are able to avoid the issue in the first place.


No. 9

"True friendship is a plant of slow growth, and must undergo and withstand the shocks of adversity,

before it is entitled to appellation." -George Washington


Why?

While some may dislike George Washington, I however find him not only a fascinating man, but also very wise. Washington understands that a good relationship between two people must undergo trials. After those trials, Washington believe then, that you are entitled to call them "Friend."


No. 8

"The measure of a man is not how many servants he has

but how many men he serves." -Albert Einstien


Why?

This is incredibly sound advice coming from incredibly bright person. Einstien understands some of the basic principles of the Bible.(Including the Golden Rule) In his quote, he recommends humbleness over pride, others before himself. That is why this quote got #8 on my list of the Top Quotes.


No. 7

"Most bad government has grown out of

too much government." -Thomas Jefferson


Why?

Every time I read this quote I can't help but chuckle a little bit. It's not only ironic, but it has so much truth to it. Jefferson was not only the chief author of the Declaration of Independance, but also a Founding Father and 3rd president of the United States, which is so fitting why he would be talking about too much government. He knew that with no government there would be lawlessness, but with too much government we would not be called "Free."

No. 6

"Our lives begin to end the day

we become silent about things that matter." -Martin Luther King JR.


Why?

I believe that it important to not lose sight of where we came from. Specifically as a country, our freedoms which we fought long and hard for,will be stripped from us unless we defend them; and we cannot defend them if we are silent.


No. 5

"What is right is not always popular

And what is popular is not always right." -Albert Einstien


Why?

This is so very true in our society. We are constanty encouraged by others to follow only what they say, and never decide to think for ourselves. It is both a dangerous and ignorant way to live.


No. 4

"Nothing can stop the man with the

right mental attitude from achieving his goals." -Thomas Jefferson

Why?

Jefferson did not say anything about achieving dreams, but did mention goals. I believe this is important because it promotes hard work and diligence to achieve a man's goals in life.


No. 3

"The time is always right

to do what is right." -Martin Luther King JR.


Why?

This deserves to be in the top three because it speaks to everyone. MLK does not make exuses for anyone nor does he back down from his beliefs.


No. 2

"Nations do not die from invasion;

they die from internal rotteness." -Abraham Lincoln


Why?

This is true of every corrupt nation. A famous one in Bible times was a great kingdom called Babylon. Babylon did not fall because of it's physical weakness, but because of its internal weaknesses. I believe this should be a warning to us, and an ecouragement to us, that our internal strenght comes from the Lord.


No. 1 Quote Of All Time-

"Do I not destroy my enemies

when I make them my friends." -Abraham Lincoln


Why?

Abraham Lincoln is many peoples favorite president. Most remember him as the guy who one the civil war, others remember he freed the slaves. I, however, remember him because of his nobilty. He was wise, honest, and above all else, a servant of the people. This quote from Lincoln spoke personally to me and hopefully to you. If we did unto others as we would have them do unto us, and everyone lived by that rule,that is a world i want to live in. That is The Top Ten Quotes of All Time.
